The best dipping sauce ever!

This is the only dipping sauce you need. Fullstop. If you only remember one thing its this. I used this for dipping, for salads as a dressing, for a marinate, its just the best.

Ingredients

  • Seaseme Oil
  • Soya Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Honey
  • (optional) Chilly flakes

Method

  1. Dump it all into a bowl and mix.

    2. I'm not joking its that simple. This is a really forgiving dressing and will always turn out fine. Fancy it up for more flavour but you'll never want to because its just so balanced already!!
