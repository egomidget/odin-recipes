Avocardo Toast

Easy, simple quick breakfast, and full of fibre, just sayingggggg

Ingredients

  • Avocardo
  • (optional) Egg
  • Bread

Method

  1. Toast bread to toast level you like

    2. I like 1 minute toast Don't judge me

  2. Start frying egg
  3. Smash avocardo on toast, buttered or unbuttered
  4. put egg on and voila, nom time
