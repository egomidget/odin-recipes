Bacon Pancakes

Bacon pancakes are tasty and not good for you. Who doesn't love that. I always suggest investing in good maple syrup over better bacon. you can pretend the pork is better, but you cannot pretend the syrup doesn't taste like plastic. Also if you do not have bacon, abit of fried ham always works in a pince - I do this more often then I would like when i just want quick pancakes :DD!!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Milk
  • Bacon
  • Oil
  • Maple syrup

Method

  1. Seive flour with a fork into a jug, add bacon to pan while you are doing this
  2. Pour little bits of milk in and stir to avoid clumps
  3. Add egg and whip to desire 'fluffyness' of pancake
  4. Pour in batter and flip when not wet anymore

    5. I ususally just make the bacon as im making the pancake but feel free to pre cook it if you are cookign for alot of people

  5. Serve with syrup and butter, eat while warm or they just aren't as good
